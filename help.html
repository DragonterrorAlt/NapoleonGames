If any of the pages look funny, follow these steps:

1. Make sure you bookmarks bar is enabled. (If it is, you will see your bookmarks at the top while on this page. If not, press CTRL + SHIFT + B to enable it)

2. Press the button. If the button make a popup with the numbers 1366x768 you are good. If not, press CTRL + SHIFT + + or CTRL + SHIFT + - and try until it is 1366x768
